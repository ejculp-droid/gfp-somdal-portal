GFP Somdal LLC | Phase III

Integration Blueprint And Operating Model Design

Phase III translates diligence findings into an executable integration design. The intent is to define how the combined company operates in practice: who owns decisions, how information moves across studios and support functions, and which controls keep delivery quality, financial discipline, and client trust consistent during scale.

Purpose And Concept

This phase establishes a coherent post-close operating narrative: one firm identity, two legacy operating traditions, and a shared execution framework. The concept is to remove ambiguity at hand-off points and leadership interfaces, then codify the future-state model as a repeatable management system.

Expected Deliverables

  • Executive integration narrative covering governance, reporting lines, and decision rights.
  • Organizational Information Flow Map for cross-functional communication paths.
  • Role Analysis Matrix defining retained, consolidated, and review-position roles.
  • Implementation checkpoint plan with ownership, milestones, and success signals.
